Our Story

A Cup Born from Passion

Mokavava was born in a small corner of the city, where the love for perfectly crafted coffee meets a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. We source our beans from the finest farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil.

Every cup is crafted with intention — from the carefully measured grind to the precise pour, we believe coffee is an art form worth perfecting.
